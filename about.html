FAQ's

Everything you need to know

DevLux Infotech offers a comprehensive range of IT and creative services including branding, UI/UX design, custom website and web application development, eCommerce solutions, mobile app development, digital marketing, SEO, and software consulting. We also provide ongoing support and maintenance to ensure long-term success for our clients.

Our clients range from startups looking to build a digital presence to established enterprises seeking advanced solutions. We work across industries such as technology, healthcare, eCommerce, real estate, education, finance, and hospitality. No matter the size or industry, we tailor our services to meet unique business goals.

Project duration varies based on complexity. A standard website might take 3–6 weeks, while more complex platforms or mobile apps can range from 8–16 weeks or more. After an initial consultation, we provide a clear project timeline tailored to your specific requirements and deliverables.

Our pricing is flexible and project-based. After understanding your goals, we create a detailed proposal outlining scope, deliverables, and cost. We offer fixed-price packages for smaller projects and custom quotes for larger or ongoing engagements. Transparent communication and no hidden costs—guaranteed.

Great UI/UX design ensures users have a smooth, intuitive, and enjoyable experience. A well-designed interface improves engagement, reduces bounce rates, and boosts conversions. At DevLux Infotech, we prioritize user research, wireframes, and usability testing to create designs that are not only beautiful but also effective in meeting user and business goals.
